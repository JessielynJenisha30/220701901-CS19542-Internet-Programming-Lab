INTRODUCTION

Rajalakshmi Engineering College, an autonomous institution affiliated to Anna University, Chennai, was established in the year 1997 under the aegis of Rajalakshmi Educational Trust whose members have had consummate experience in the fields of education and industry. The College has grown from strength to strength in the last 25 years and progressing towards Excellence in Engineering Education, Research and Development.

Vision

To be an institution of excellence in Engineering, Technology and Management Education & Research. To provide competent and ethical professionals with a concern for society.

Mission

To impart quality technical education imbibed with proficiency and humane values. To provide right ambience and opportunities for the students to develop into creative, talented and globally competent professionals. To promote research and development in technology and management for the benefit of the society.

OPPORTUNITIES

Why do Corporates prefer our students?

  • Quality Input: 50% of the students are admitted through Anna University State level single window system; Balance 50% of students are admitted by merit through Management quota.
  • Strong in academics: Good number of University Ranks and Academic Performance reveals the students' strength in academic knowledge. Well qualified and experienced faculty with an appreciating.
  • Faculty: student ratio of 1:13.1 delivers sound knowledge; Appreciable no.of PhD holders among faculty helps students to take part in research projects.
  • Vibrant Co-curriculars: Well stocked library with online journals, 24X7 internet, Professional clubs and seminars help our students to win prizes in National Level Project and Paper presentation competitions. Few got jobs through their projects in companies like Mahindra & Mahindra.
  • Value addition: Current technical and practical knowledge is added through Guest Lectures, In Plant Training, Industrial Visits and Industry Projects. On-campus certification programmes in SUN JAVA, Microsoft Certified Programmes and CATIA.
  • Skill enhancement: Soft skill and employability skills are enhanced through training camps. Students are of PLUG & PLAY type to deliver results from Day 1.
  • Overall development: Campus atmosphere provides platform for overall development of students in academics, co & extra-curriculars, socialize, organize, lead and leap in their walks of life.

Excellent performance and elevation at job by our alumni shows that the students are groomed well during their college days.
