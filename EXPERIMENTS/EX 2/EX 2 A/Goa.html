Natural Resources of Goa

  • Iron Ore: Goa is rich in iron ore deposits, which are primarily used in the steel industry. It is one of the largest iron ore producers in India.
  • Manganese: The state also has significant deposits of manganese, used in steel production and other industrial applications.
  • Bauxite: Bauxite, the raw material for aluminum production, is found in substantial quantities in Goa.
  • Forests: Goa's forests are rich in biodiversity and provide timber, medicinal plants, and other forest products.
  • Water Resources: Major rivers like the Mandovi and Zuari flow through Goa, supporting agriculture and providing drinking water.
  • Beaches and Tourism: While not a traditional natural resource, Goa's beautiful beaches are a vital part of its economy, attracting millions of tourists every year.
